資訊增益(Infomation Gain)¶

  • I(p1p2)
    • p1S$p1\over S$
    • p1S1C1p1S2C2
    • p1p1$p1\_I(S1,S2) = I(C1,C2) = -{{C1}\over p1}log{{C1}\over p1}-{{C2}\over p1}log{{C2}\over p1}$
    • p2$p2\_I(S1,S2)$
  • $$E() = {p1\over S} \times p1\_I(S1,S2)+{p1\over S}\times p1\_I(S1,S2)$$
  • $$Information\ Gain() = S_I(S1,S2) - E()$$
